Subramanian Sundaram

Boston University & Wyss Institute, Harvard University.


I am a Postdoctoral Fellow exploring the interface of Robotics and Tissue Engineering. I work with Prof. Chris Chen at Boston University and the Wyss Institute for Biologically Inspired Engineering at Harvard University.

I received my PhD from MIT where I worked with Prof. Wojciech Matusik at the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Lab (CSAIL).

Previously, I received my undergraduate degrees in Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering at BITS Pilani, India. I did my undergraduate theses with Prof. Herbert Shea at EPFL, Switzerland and Prof. Klaus Kern at the Max Planck Institute for Solid State Research, Germany.
